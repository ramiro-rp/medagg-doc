# ADR-0000 – Architecture Decision Record Template

- Status: draft
- Date: 2025-10-01
- Owners: Team Lead, Backend Dev
- Related meetings: weekly Monday engineering meetings

## Context

Use this template when documenting an architecture or technology decision
that significantly affects the system.

## Decision

Short one-sentence summary of the decision.

## Rationale

Why this decision was made, including key arguments, constraints and
trade-offs.

## Alternatives considered

- Alternative A – short description, pros / cons
- Alternative B – short description, pros / cons

## Consequences

- Positive effects
- Potential risks and downsides
- Impact on other parts of the system (backend, search module, frontend,
  deployment, operations)

## References

- Links to transcripts, issues, PRs, external documents.
